Proprietorship

A sole proprietorship, also known as an individual entrepreneurship, is a type of enterprise owned and run by one person. A sole proprietorship is the simplest and most common structure chosen to start a business. In this form of business the proprietor is solely and wholly responsible for his/her act and liability is unlimited.

The proprietorship registration process involves obtaining a PAN card, opening a current bank account in the name of the business, and obtaining other necessary licenses and permits based on the type of business activity. It is important to note that a proprietorship firm does not have a separate legal entity and is not considered a separate entity from its owner. Therefore, the proprietor is personally responsible for all the liabilities of the business.
