One Person Company

If you are a sole proprietor or a single owner looking to start your own business, registering your company as a One Person Company (OPC) can be an ideal option. OPC is a legal structure that allows a single person to register a company and enjoy the benefits of a Private Limited Company without needing to have a partner.

Unlock the potential of your solo business dreams with One Person Company (OPC) - let us help you take the first step towards success!

What is OPC ?

A One Person Company (OPC) is a type of company that has only one shareholder. It is introduced by the Companies Act, 2013, to provide a business structure that allows entrepreneurs to start and operate their business on their own while enjoying the benefits of limited liability.It motivates the small traders and entrepreneurs who have the potential to start their own business and build up their own identity.

Frequently Asked Questions

A One Person Company (OPC) is a type of private limited company that can be formed with just one director and one shareholder.

Only an Indian resident and citizen can register a One Person Company (OPC). The person must also have a valid PAN card and be at least 18 years old.

There is no minimum capital requirement for registering a One Person Company (OPC). The company can be registered with a nominal capital of just Rs. 1 Lakh.
